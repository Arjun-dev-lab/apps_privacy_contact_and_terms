Features of the App

The app offers the following features:

  • Tracking and updating periods completed or not.
  • Class timetable management and reminders, including local notifications.
  • Viewing textbooks and handbooks through Google Drive links integrated via WebView.
  • Attendance management, including the ability to download attendance data locally.

Permissions Used

The app requires the following permissions for its functionality:

  • Internet Access: To load external resources, Google Drive links, and advertisements.
  • Access Network State: To check network connectivity status.
  • Access WiFi State: To monitor WiFi connectivity.
  • Exact Alarm Scheduling: To schedule accurate reminders.
  • Post Notifications: To send reminders, updates, and policy changes via push notifications.
  • Read/Write External Storage: To save and download attendance data.
  • Manage External Storage: To handle file access when downloading attendance data.
  • Firebase AD_ID: For personalized advertisements via the Meta Audience Network.

Data collected by Firebase and Meta is subject to their respective privacy policies and cannot be deleted by users. Attendance records downloaded from the app are stored locally on the user's device and are not shared externally by the app.
