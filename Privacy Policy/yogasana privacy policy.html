Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January, 2025

Welcome to Yogasana

Thank you for using Yogasana, a yoga app focused on helping you practice Suryanamaskar effectively. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and stored to ensure transparency and your peace of mind. Please read it carefully.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

To provide you with a personalized experience, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name: Displayed within the app to personalize your experience.
  • Age: Used for tracking and displaying your fitness progress.
  • Height and Weight: Used to calculate your BMI (Body Mass Index).
  • Profile Picture: Stored locally on your device to create your profile.

Activity Data

We store your yoga activity and performance history locally on your device to help you track your progress over time.

How Your Information Is Used

We use the collected data to:

  • Personalize your app experience.
  • Calculate BMI based on your height and weight.
  • Help you track your yoga performance and progress.
  • Send notifications through Firebase Cloud Messaging.

Local Data Storage

All your personal and activity data, such as profile details and yoga performance history, is stored locally on your device. You have complete control over this data and can manage or delete it through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services to enhance our app functionality:

  • Firebase Analytics: Tracks app usage patterns and helps us improve the user experience. Analytics data is not stored locally and cannot be deleted by users.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Enables push notifications for reminders and updates.

Data Sharing

Your personal data is not shared with third parties except as required for the functionality of Firebase services. We do not sell, rent, or disclose your data to advertisers or other entities.

Data Deletion

You can manage or delete your data in the following ways:

  • Clear all app data through your device settings.
  • Delete specific activity history directly from the app interface.

Note: Firebase Analytics data cannot be deleted as it is managed by Google and used for improving app performance.

ASK Productions does not assume any responsibility for monitoring user age or for any issues arising from usage by individuals under the age of 18.

Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. While we strive to safeguard your information, no system is entirely secure. We encourage you to use your device responsibly and ensure it is protected.
