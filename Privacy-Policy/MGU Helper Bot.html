Privacy Policy for MGU Helper

Introduction

Welcome to the MGU Helper! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide while using the MGU Helper. By interacting with MGU Helper, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

The MGU Helper may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you interact with the app, you may be asked to provide your name, email address, student ID, or other details required to assist with your academic or administrative queries.
  • Interaction Data: We log data about your interactions with the app, including questions asked, responses provided, and specific actions you take during the conversation.
  • Device and Technical Information: The app may collect information about the device you use to interact with it, such as device type, operating system, browser type, IP address, and geographical location (based on your IP address).
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We may use cookies and similar technologies to analyze how users engage with the app and enhance the user experience. These technologies help us improve functionality and performance.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To Assist You: The primary purpose of collecting data is to assist you with your academic or administrative inquiries, such as providing information about courses, exam schedules, campus events, and more.
  • To Improve the Service: We analyze usage patterns to identify areas where the app can be improved. This includes enhancing its ability to understand and respond to user queries more accurately.
  • To Ensure Security and Fraud Prevention: The data we collect helps us ensure that the app is used appropriately and securely, protecting it from abuse and fraud.
  • To Communicate with You: We may use your contact information (if provided) to send updates, notifications, or reminders about services or events related to Mahatma Gandhi University (MGU).

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. If you wish to delete any personal data or stop using the app, you can contact us to request data deletion.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide to the MGU Helper. These measures include:

  • Encryption: Sensitive data, such as login credentials, is encrypted during transmission.
  • Access Control: Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Data Storage: Data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties unless necessary for providing services or fulfilling legal obligations.

Third-Party Services

We may share your information with third-party services only in the following situations:

  • To Improve the Service: We may use third-party analytics services to improve the app’s performance. These services do not collect personally identifiable information.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes, such as a court order or government request.

Third-Party Integrations (Wikipedia API)

The MGU Helper app integrates with the Wikipedia API to provide educational content. By using this feature, you agree to the terms and conditions of the Wikipedia API. The data you access from Wikipedia is publicly available, and we are not responsible for the content provided through the API. Please be aware that Wikipedia’s Privacy Policy governs the collection and use of your data when you interact with the app's Wikipedia-powered features.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to the information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correct: You can request corrections or updates to your data.
  • Right to Delete: You can request to delete your personal data from our systems. However, this may impact the functionality of the app.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If we are processing your data based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

Children's Privacy

The MGU Helper is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove that data.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance the user experience with the MGU Helper. These tools help us analyze user behavior and optimize the app’s performance. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect some features of the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the services we offer or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date.
